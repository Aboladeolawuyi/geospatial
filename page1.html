Our Team

Surv. Damilola Kolawole
MD / Lead Solution Architect

A seasoned solution expert with over 17 years of delivering innovative projects in the geospatial and IT space. A Registered Surveyor, Oracle-Certified Database Expert, and award-winning leader, Damilola has built a reputation for excellence, vision, and innovation. As the founder and driving force behind Geospatial Information Resource Limited, he combines technical mastery with strategic leadership, steering the company toward groundbreaking solutions that empower clients and transform industries.

Abolade Olawuyi
Project Manager / Front End Developer

A results-driven professional with a Master’s degree in Surveying and Geoinformatics, Abolade blends technical skill with strategic project management. At Geospatial Information Resource Limited, he oversees projects that unite IT innovation and surveying expertise, delivering solutions in software engineering, GIS applications, and precise land surveying. His focus on efficiency, quality, and client satisfaction ensures every project meets the highest standards.

Habib Abdulsalam
Process Manager / DBA

A Flexiblebase-certified Database Administrator with an HND in Surveying and Geoinformatics, Habib specializes in managing and optimizing data systems that power our geospatial and IT solutions. At Geospatial Information Resource Limited, he oversees operational workflows and ensures that spatial and non-spatial databases are secure, efficient, and reliable. His combined expertise in surveying and database administration allows him to bridge field data collection with robust back-end data management, supporting projects from acquisition to delivery.
