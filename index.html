Geospatial Solutions

Geospatial Information Resource Limited is your trusted partner for innovative IT solutions and professional surveying services. Since 2010, we have been delivering excellence in software engineering, cloud computing, data analytics, and geospatial intelligence while providing precise cadastral, topographic, GIS, and engineering surveys. By blending digital transformation with geospatial expertise, we help businesses, governments, and organizations make smarter decisions, optimize operations, and achieve measurable results.
