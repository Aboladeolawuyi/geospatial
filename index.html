Geospatial Solutions

Geospatial Information Resource Limited is your trusted partner for innovative IT solutions and professional surveying services. Since 2010, we have been delivering excellence in software engineering, cloud computing, data analytics, and geospatial intelligence while providing precise cadastral, topographic, GIS, and engineering surveys. By blending digital transformation with geospatial expertise, we help businesses, governments, and organizations make smarter decisions, optimize operations, and achieve measurable results.

Our Services

IT & Digital Transformation Solutions

We design and deliver advanced technology solutions, including software engineering, data analytics & machine learning, and cloud & big data services. Our expertise helps businesses modernize operations, unlock insights from their data, and achieve sustainable growth.

Surveying & Geoinformatics Services

Our licensed surveyors provide professional cadastral, topographic, engineering, and hydrographic surveys, along with layout design for estates and developments. We ensure accuracy, compliance, and reliability in all land measurement and mapping projects.

Enterprise GIS Software Development

We develop Enterprise GIS Software, including asset mapping, spatial data analysis, and location-based decision support. Our solutions help organizations visualize, interpret, and manage spatial information for smarter planning and resource management.

Engineering Services

We deliver comprehensive engineering services spanning civil, structural, electrical, and architectural design and implementation. Our integrated team collaborates to plan, design, and execute projects with precision, efficiency, and innovation, ensuring high-quality results from concept to completion.
